Padezhtropos

Napaksu

Inne afto vipaperi, un kaku oba fal fu padezhtropos ka mange simpljena russossatropos kara, au deki brukjena prosta per antaa chigaunen asmi fraz made. Bruktropos je tak, ka per tingko, prosta kawari tel fu kotoba per hanu natsatain, ke ko jam na joku fal inne fraz. Tuo ke, afto bai chigausejena kotoba na surufal au ko na andrfal. Tatoeba, “kot” na surufal deki, au “kota” deki na andrfal – tabun jenafal, os harfal. Ka atai padezh brukjena, au ka pitka made, un shirunai – tabun, ti haaste lera mange chigau fal fu kotoba, au ende vi nai treng – de, ka plusnintendo au plus smaklik, plusbra. Iskatda inne viossa fdu, li vil.

Apar dekilik hadjipie

Padezhtropos deki mange simpel, os-au har mange chigau tel. Per mahklar tulla-tatoeba, un brukmirai fraz, ka har go tingko ka deki inne chigau fal:

Un nosí ór fu kot hant made unna stol
